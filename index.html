Biography

Welcome to my personal page! I am a research assistant at the Dept. of Decision Sciences at Bocconi University. Broadly speaking, I am interested in the intersection between Bayesian Inference and Probabilistic Machine Learning with a particular focus on Bayesian Computation. Currently, I am working on developing a novel stochastic gradient MCMC.

In october ‘21, I completed a MSc in Data Science at Bocconi University advised by Prof. Giacomo Zanella, with a thesis on stochastic gradient MCMC. Prior to that, I obtained a BSc in Economics, where I focused on economic modeling.

Feel free to contact me at lorenzo[dot]mauri[at]studbocconi[dot]it.
